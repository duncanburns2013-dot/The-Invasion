## METHODOLOGY

1. Downloaded complete MA State Checkbook for Executive Office of Housing
2. Filtered vendors by immigrant-related names/missions
3. Separated MassHealth billing from actual program spending
4. Integrated official state shelter cost reports
5. Applied CIS study estimates for education ($23K/student) and healthcare ($3,800/person)
6. Cross-referenced contract recipients with lobbyist filings
7. Analyzed OCPF records for ACLU employee political contributions
